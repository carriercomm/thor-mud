Available commands are:
  help           - Get this message.
  look           - See the room you are in.
  quit (exit)    - Leave this boring place.
  say            - Say something to anyone currently in the same room as you.
                   ex: say What's up doc?
  shout          - Say something to everyone in the game.
  tell (whisper) - Tell something to a specific player.
  who (list)     - Show all of the players currently logged in.
